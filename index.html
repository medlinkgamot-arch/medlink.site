Our Mission

Unlocking new opportunities for every Filipino pharmacy.

MEDLINK helps pharmacy operators unlock new opportunities by converting their existing businesses into a UHC‑GAMOT facility delivering greater value to patients, local government units, and healthcare providers alike.

We don’t just help pharmacies comply. We help them evolve into vital healthcare partners that improve medicine access, strengthen local healthcare systems, and create lasting value for patients and communities.

The MEDLINK Pathway

From existing pharmacy to accredited primary care partner (UHC-GAMOT Facility).

A structured, end-to-end engagement with each stage building the foundation for the next.

  1. I

    Feasibility & readiness assessment

    Feasibility assessment and pharmacy readiness evaluation, paired with business planning and sustainability review.

  2. II

    Facility planning

    Facility planning and workflow optimization designed around day-to-day pharmacy operations.

  3. III

    Accreditation & documentation

    Accreditation and documentary assistance with regulatory agencies, handled from start to finish.

  4. IV

    Standard operating procedures

    Development of standard operating procedures (SOPs) that keep every process consistent and compliant.

  5. V

    Inventory & medicines planning

    Inventory management and essential medicines planning that keeps the right stock on the shelf.

  6. VI

    Local health system integration

    Onboarding and integration with local health systems and Primary Care Provider Networks (PCPNs).

  7. VII

    Continuous technical assistance

    Ongoing technical assistance and quality improvement, so the facility keeps evolving after go-live.

Every stage strengthens medicine access and the local healthcare system it serves.
